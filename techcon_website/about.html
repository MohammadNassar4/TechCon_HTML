History

Origins: The Birth of TechCon

The genesis of TechCon can be traced back to the early 1990s, with the establishment of the Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC). SRC's TECHCON conference was initiated to bridge the gap between academia and industry, providing a platform for students and professionals to present research and innovations in semiconductor technology. The inaugural event, held in 1990, set the stage for an annual tradition that emphasized high-quality presentations and industry relevance.

The Modern Era: Virtual and Hybrid Formats

The COVID-19 pandemic necessitated a shift towards virtual platforms. TechCon events adapted by embracing digital formats, ensuring continued knowledge exchange despite global disruptions. For instance, ARM's DevSummit transitioned to online-only conferences in 2020 and 2021, maintaining its commitment to showcasing new hardware, software, and technologies using ARM processors.
